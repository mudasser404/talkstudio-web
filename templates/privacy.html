Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 4, 2025

At TalkStudio.ai, your privacy is our priority. We are committed to protecting the personal information of our users and ensuring transparency in how we handle data.

Information We Collect

How We Use Your Information

Data Security

We use SSL encryption and other security measures to protect your data. Only authorized personnel can access user information when necessary for support or maintenance.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services (such as payment processors and analytics providers). Each third party follows strict data protection standards.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information by contacting us at support@talkstudio.ai.
